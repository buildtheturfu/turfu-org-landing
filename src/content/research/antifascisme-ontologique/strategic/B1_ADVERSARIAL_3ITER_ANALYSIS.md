## Why iteration 3 yielded only marginal movement

The hostile reviewer's v3 critique converges on a single structural finding that **cannot be resolved without changing the paper's nature**:

> "SRBS cannot referee a paper whose central non-triviality witness is unpublished and unavailable. […] Either include the 16-state Rosen construction and the separation lemma inline, or recast the paper as fully self-standing without reference to the companion."

This is the salami-slicing tension the cover letter (¶3) explicitly addresses, in mirror image:
- **Inline the finite model** → resolves the falsifiability gap, but increases overlap with BioSystems → risks ICMJE/COPE redundant-publication concern
- **Don't inline** → preserves the triptyque declension, but leaves the structural claim load-bearing on a forthcoming paper SRBS reviewers cannot inspect

Both moves have legitimate costs. The "right" answer depends on whether one optimises for SRBS-reviewer-comfort (inline) or for ICMJE-COPE-cleanliness (don't inline). The cover letter took the second path.

## What the adversarial reviewer would NOT accept regardless of further fixes

Three findings persist across iterations 2 and 3, suggesting they are structural rather than fixable:

1. **The empirical thesis remains supported by 3 illustrative cases, not coded data.** A coded survey against Polity IV / V-Dem / Freedom House would close this — but that's a separate empirical paper, not a fix to B1.

2. **The "three independent structural realizations" still operates as analogy without an explicit functor.** Supplying the functor would require Markov categories machinery that the BioSystems paper itself defers to a follow-up. So the only way to harden this is to wait for Paper 2 in the programme.

3. **The convergence with property/non-aggression in §5.3-5.4 will read as libertarian-tilt to allergic SRBS reviewers** even with the four-completions disclaimer. Working ALL four completions (Ostrom, Habermas, Pettit) in operational parity would make the paper double in length and lose its center.

These are the inherent costs of B1's chosen role in the triptyque (systems bridge, not formal core, not full normative theory).
